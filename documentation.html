How To hire helpers ......

Step 1: Go To hiring Block
Step 2: Click on Helpers like maid,cook,driver....
Step 3: Then Employee Details will open Choose any Employee Which You want to hire.
Step 4: Fill The required Details in the Form.
Step 5 :Pay The amount.
Step 6:You have successfully Hire the helper.

How To hire Repairs ......

Step 1: Go To Repairing Block
Step 2: Click on repairs like painter,gardener....
Step 3: Then Employee Details will open Choose any Employee Which You want to hire.
Step 4: Fill The required Details in the Form.
Step 5 :Pay The amount.
Step 6:You have successfully Hire the Repairs.

How To Apply For Job ......

Step 1: Go To job Block
Step 2: Click on apply For Job Button
Step 3: Fill The required Details in the Form.
Step 4 :Click on apply Button
Step 5:You have successfully Apply for job We will Contact You.
